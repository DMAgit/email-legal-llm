# Approved Clause Library

## Approved Liability Clause

Each party's aggregate liability is limited to fees paid or payable under the
agreement in the twelve months before the claim. Neither party is liable for
indirect, incidental, special, consequential, or punitive damages, except for
confidentiality breaches and payment obligations.

## Non-Approved Liability Clause

The customer is liable for all direct, indirect, incidental, consequential, and
punitive damages without limitation. This conflicts with the internal liability
cap policy and must escalate to legal review.

## Approved Data Usage Clause

Vendor may use customer data only to provide and support the services, maintain
security, comply with law, and create aggregated usage metrics that do not
identify the customer or any individual.

## Prohibited Data Usage Clause

Vendor may use customer data to train machine learning models, improve
unrelated products, or share analytics with third parties. This conflicts with
the internal data usage policy and requires legal review.

## Approved Termination Clause

Either party may terminate for material breach if the breach is not cured
within 30 days after written notice. Customer may terminate for convenience on
30 days written notice for hosted services.

## Approved Payment Clause

Invoices are due Net 30 after receipt of a valid invoice. Fees are billed in
arrears unless an order form expressly approves another schedule.
