# Contract Review Policy

## Liability

Standard vendor contracts should cap aggregate liability at fees paid in the
previous twelve months. Unlimited liability, uncapped indemnity, consequential
damages exposure, or vendor-only liability caps require legal review.

## Data Usage

Vendors may process customer data only to provide contracted services. Training
AI models on customer data, selling customer data, or using customer data for
unrelated product development is prohibited unless legal has approved explicit
language.

## Payment Terms

Preferred payment terms are Net 30 or Net 45 after receipt of a valid invoice.
Net 60 is non-standard and requires procurement review. Prepayment over 25% of
annual fees requires procurement approval.

## Termination

Contracts should include termination for material breach with a cure period of
30 days or less. Termination for convenience is preferred for SaaS tools that
handle customer data. Vendor-only termination rights require legal review.

## Renewal

Automatic renewal is acceptable only when non-renewal notice is 60 days or
less and renewal pricing is capped or clearly disclosed. Longer notice periods
or uncapped renewal price increases require procurement review.

## Governing Law

Company-preferred governing law is Delaware, New York, or the company's home
jurisdiction. Foreign governing law or exclusive venue outside approved
jurisdictions requires legal review.
