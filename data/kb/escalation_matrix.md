# Escalation Matrix

## Legal Review

Send to legal review when retrieved context identifies unlimited liability,
uncapped indemnity, prohibited data usage, foreign governing law, missing
privacy protections, or vendor-only termination rights.

## Procurement Review

Send to procurement review for Net 60 payment terms, annual prepayment above
25%, uncapped renewal price increases, automatic renewal notice periods over
60 days, or non-standard commercial terms.

## Manual Review

Send to manual review when extraction confidence is low, retrieval returns no
policy context, key clauses are missing, or the classifier finds contradictory
evidence.

## Auto Store

Auto store only when all key terms align with retrieved policy, no conflicts
are present, extraction confidence is strong, and the classifier confidence is
high.
